# Welcome to the Lore Sanctum Wiki 🎲

**Lore Sanctum** is a coordinated AI agent ecosystem designed to act as your **RPG Dungeon Master's "Second Brain"**. 

Unlike traditional AI chat interfaces where knowledge is scattered and ephemeral, Lore Sanctum actively compiles your campaign into a structured, interconnected, and immutable database (Obsidian-style). It eliminates inconsistencies, tracks plot holes, and drastically reduces cognitive load for the DM.

---

## 🏛️ The "Second Brain" Philosophy

Lore Sanctum is built on the principle of **Compilation over Retrieval**. 

Instead of asking an AI to "remember" what happened 10 sessions ago (and hoping it doesn't hallucinate), the system maintains a three-tier architecture in your campaign folder:

1.  **`raw/` (The Inbox):** Where session logs, informal notes, and rule PDFs live. These are immutable data sources.
2.  **`wiki/` (The Encyclopedia):** Perfectly formatted Markdown pages for NPCs, Factions, and Locations. Uses bit-directional `[[Links]]` to create a visual **Knowledge Graph** (fully compatible with [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/)).
3.  **`schema/` (The World Laws):** Formatting rules that ensure agents keep the Wiki consistent and professional.
