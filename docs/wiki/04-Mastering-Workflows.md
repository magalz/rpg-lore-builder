# Mastering Workflows 🛠️

Workflows (or "Rituals") are guided journeys that coordinate multiple agents to achieve a complex goal.

## 📝 Session Log Workflow
**When to use:** Immediately after a game session.
Don't worry about writing a perfect summary. Just "dump" your messy notes or a rough recap.
1.  **Discovery:** The workflow agent will interview you briefly to fill in the blanks (Who died? What loot was found?).
2.  **Archiving:** It saves a clean report in your `raw/` folder.
3.  **Ingestion:** The **Historian** is automatically summoned to update your Wiki based on the new session events.

## 🗓️ Prep Session Workflow
**When to use:** When you sit down to plan your next game.
1.  **Auditing:** The **Inquisitor** delivers the "Morning Audit" (reminders of plot holes and loose ends).
2.  **Drama:** The **Weaver** proposes narrative beats for the next session.
3.  **Drafting:** Together, you draft a session outline.
4.  **Crunch:** The **Tactician** joins at the end to provide the monsters and mechanics needed for your draft.

## 🧬 Genesis Workflow (Session 0)
**When to use:** Starting a brand new campaign.
It helps you define the scope, tone, and foundational lore (The "Backlog Lore"). It ensures your Wiki starts with a healthy structure.

## 💎 Party Knowledge (Handout)
**When to use:** When players ask, "Wait, what do we know about this faction?"
The agent filters out the "DM Only" secrets from the Wiki and generates a clean summary that you can safely export and send to your players.
