# The Lore Sentinels 🛡️

While the Council creates, the Sentinels ensure your world remains **Consistent, Logical, and Organized**. They work primarily in the background.

## 📜 The Historian (`rlb-agent-historian`)
**Role:** The Compiler & Wiki Maintainer.
The Historian is the bridge between your informal session "dumps" and the professional Wiki. It performs **Universal Ingestion**.
*   **How it works:** When you finish a session log, the Historian reads the raw text, identifies new NPCs/Locations, updates existing notes, and creates bi-directional links (`[[Like This]]`).
*   **Autonomous Power:** The Historian can create multiple files and folders in your campaign directory in a single turn.

## 🔍 The Inquisitor (`rlb-agent-inquisitor`)
**Role:** Canon Auditor & Plot Hole Hunter.
The Inquisitor is the "Quality Control" of your campaign. It acts in two vital ways:

1.  **The Canon Filter (Reactive):** When you try to add new lore that contradicts the past (e.g., saying an NPC is alive when they died in session 2), the Inquisitor will flag the inconsistency and ask for a fix.
2.  **The Morning Audit (Proactive):** Before you start planning a session, the Inquisitor scans the entire Wiki for "Dead Ends" or "Forgotten Hooks".
    *   *Example Alert:* "DM, you left NPC Kael in a dungeon 3 months ago. The players haven't mentioned him. Should his faction declare him dead?"
